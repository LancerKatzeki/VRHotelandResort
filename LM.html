Hotel Services/Facility

Room Service:

Using room service in VR means you can enjoy the city's attraction day and night without having to worry about timings for eating or drinking.In-room dining at VR's Hotel and Resort is available 24 hours a day, bringing the same high levels of service and culinary experience found in the hotel's restaurant and bars to the privacy of a room or suite.

Restaurant:

At VR' restaurant, dining options are designed with you in mind. Start the day with a hearty breakfast or nutritious light bite; savour innovative dishes prepared with only the freshest ingredients, or simply relax with a cup of coffee in the hotel lounge. From business brunches to pre-dinner drinks and everything in between, the options are catered especially for you.
HOURS:
Monday to Sunday
Breakfast: 6:00 AM - 10:30 AM Dinner: 6:00 PM - 10:30 PM Monday to Friday Lunch: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Saturday to Sunday Brunch: 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM

Bars:

VR's Bars offers seven bars throughout the resort – including a swim-up bar. At each bar, the Caribbean vibe comes to life with the energy of our star bartenders as they serve up an assortment of tropical blends from fruit smoothies and mixed drinks to colorful cocktails or Piton, the local beer. All of our bars offer a wide selection of premium liquors. Ask about the signature “Cocktail of the Day.
